Introduction

Welcome to Typebot. Typebot is a web application designed to help businesses create custom chatbot solutions to streamline their processes. We understand the importance of your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services, including our use of the Google Sheets API.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect depend on how you use Typebot. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Information you provide directly to us when you create an account, configure your chatbot, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, and business information.
  • Chatbot Data: Information collected through the chatbots you create using Typebot. This may include user interactions with the chatbot, responses provided, and any data submitted by users through the chatbot.
  • Google Sheets Data: If you integrate your chatbot with Google Sheets using our Google Sheets API integration, we may access and process data from your specified Google Sheets as authorized by you. This access is solely for the purpose of facilitating the functionality of your chatbot as configured by you. We do not store your Google Sheets data on our servers beyond what is necessary for the immediate operation of the chatbot integration.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use Typebot, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, and the dates and times of your visits.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain Typebot and its features.
  • To create and manage your account.
  • To enable you to create and customize chatbots.
  • To facilitate the integration with Google Sheets and process data as authorized by you for your chatbot's functionality.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand Typebot.
  • To understand and analyze how you use Typebot.
  • To communicate with you, including for customer service, updates, and promotional purposes.
  • To detect and prevent fraud and other illegal activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
